Visit Site ↗

Africa has incredible places to stay. The problem has always been finding them. That's the whole idea behind AccoLink. A platform built specifically for this continent guesthouses from Botswana to Kenya to Egypt and beyond.

Under the hood, Accolink runs on Supabase handling authentication, real-time database queries, and row-level security policies for multi-tenant property management. WhatsApp and email notification integrations keep guests and property owners in sync at every stage of the booking. The frontend is built in plain HTML/CSS/JS for maximum compatibility and performance.

The site is clean, focused, and does exactly what it needs to do. You land on it, search your destination anywhere across Africa, set your budget in USD, and the listings come up. No clutter, no noise. Just find your stay and get there.
