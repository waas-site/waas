Refund Policy

Effective Date: 27 June 2026  ·  WaaS (Website as a Service)  ·  Botswana

Deposits

All deposits paid to WaaS are non-refundable. Once a deposit is made, we begin allocating time, resources, and planning toward your project. The deposit covers this initial work regardless of whether the project reaches completion.

Full Payments

If you have made a full payment before work has begun and you cancel within 48 hours of payment, you are entitled to a full refund.

After 48 hours, the deposit portion (50%) is non-refundable, and the remaining 50% will be assessed based on how much work has been completed.

Mid-Project Cancellations

If you choose to cancel a project after work has begun:

  • The deposit is non-refundable in all cases
  • Any work completed up to the point of cancellation will be assessed, and a partial refund of the remaining balance may be issued at WaaS's discretion based on the stage of completion
  • If more than 50% of the project is complete at the time of cancellation, no refund will be issued on the remaining balance

Disputes

If you are unhappy with the work delivered, please contact us first. We are committed to resolving issues fairly and will offer revisions within the original project scope before any refund is considered.

Refunds will not be issued on the basis of change of mind, change of direction, or dissatisfaction with elements that were approved during the revision process.

Template Sales

All template sales are final and non-refundable. Because digital products are delivered immediately and cannot be returned, we do not offer refunds on any template purchases. Please review all information carefully before purchasing.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or raise a dispute, contact us via WhatsApp or email with your project details and the reason for your request. We will respond within 3 business days.
